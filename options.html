FAQ

  • How to use Simple Gmail Notes (SGN)?
  • After installation, simply refresh the Gmail page (presss enter in address bar) to activate the extension.
    Then click into any email, and click the 'login' url above the text area.
    After login, you could type the notes in the text area, the notes will be saved automatically.
    For more detailed instructions, open this options page any time via the extension popup.
  • How do I view the notes on mobile devices?
  • Chrome extensions and Firefox add-ons cannot run directly on mobile browsers.
    SGN supports mobile access through the optional mobile sync integration. Enable or disable the share button in the preferences above depending on whether you operate a compatible companion service.
  • Will SGN be affected if I use the mobile integration?
  • No. Your existing notes, services, and privacy settings remain the same. The integration only adds the ability to synchronise notes with an external companion service.
  • When and where are the notes saved?
  • By default, the notes are saved whenever the text area is out of focus (e.g. click anywhere else on the page, or view another email).
    The notes are saved in your own Google drive. Click the magnifier glass above the SGN textarea and then click the Google drive icon, you should see the storage folder
  • May I get more information about the extension?
  • Refer to the project README bundled with the extension source for additional documentation.
  • Why I can't see the note abstract?
  • Some times the note abstract would take up to 2 seconds to appear. If the abstract does not appear after a long time, please help report a bug.
  • Why I need to log into my Google drive again while I already opened Google drive in another tab?
  • For security reasons, the extension could not get the Google drive access from your existing browser right away. You need to explicitly grant so.
